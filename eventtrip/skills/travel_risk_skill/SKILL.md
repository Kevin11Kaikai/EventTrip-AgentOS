# Travel Risk Skill

Use this skill to identify practical risks in an event trip before travelers spend money.

## Risks To Check

- Official ticket availability versus resale risk
- Hotel cancellation policy
- Backup lodging options
- Shared-room boundaries and confirmed bed count
- Same-day flight delay risk
- Arrival time and late-night safety
- Local transportation surge pricing
- One traveler backing out
- Budget creep from optional upgrades

## Process

1. Prefer official resale or verified transfer paths when possible.
2. Avoid same-day arrival if a flight delay could cause a missed event.
3. Book refundable hotel inventory while ticket timing is uncertain.
4. Confirm two beds for shared rooms.
5. Create a backup plan for local transportation after the event.
6. Keep individual flights and tickets separate.
7. Document AA expectations before shared bookings.

## Output Format

- Risk register
- Severity
- Mitigation
- Owner
- Decision deadline
